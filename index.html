About

Agriculture, or agro, refers to the cultivation of plants and the raising of animals for food, fiber, medicinal plants, and other products used to sustain and enhance human life. It is the backbone of most economies, providing the primary source of food and raw materials for industries. Agriculture involves various activities such as crop production, livestock farming, agroforestry, aquaculture, and horticulture. Over the centuries, advancements in technology and farming techniques have increased productivity, helping to meet the demands of growing populations. Modern agriculture emphasizes sustainable practices, such as crop rotation, organic farming, and soil conservation, to maintain soil health and reduce environmental impact. Additionally, the sector plays a critical role in rural development, employment, and economic stability, especially in developing regions. By focusing on innovations like precision farming.
